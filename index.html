08 — GENDER ATTITUDES

The political context looks internally coherent: interpersonal trust is low everywhere, corruption perceptions are high, and democratic commitment varies meaningfully by country. This matters for messenger selection. Messages that sound elite, institutional, or scolding may underperform; credible community and peer messengers are likely important.

11 — COUNTRY IMPLICATIONS

Country Patterns Should Guide Phase 2 Emphasis

ARGENTINA
  • More egalitarian on provider and gender-role items, but feminism backlash remains meaningful.
  • Lower TikTok use; La Derecha Diario recognition is highest.
COLOMBIA
  • Highest manosphere familiarity and high social-media political influence (46%).
  • Recover-masculinity and masculinity-crisis themes have notable reach.
CHILE
  • Politically fragmented environment with lower democracy top-box and meaningful gender gaps.
  • Country strategy should avoid assuming a single ideological lane.
HONDURAS
  • Strongest grievance signals around feminism and masculinity under attack, plus highest TikTok use (78%).
  • Interpret directionally because n=352.
Recommended Phase 2 design: report total and country-specific message results; use gender/age subgroup reads only where N supports it.
